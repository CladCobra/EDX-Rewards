How It Works

Our rewards system rewards users with points for practicing problems and provides them with an incentive to strive for better performance. Points are proportional to accuracy and can be redeemed for currency in video games or other items. This system promotes a sense of accomplishment and incentivizes users to continue learning. It creates a positive feedback loop and is a powerful tool for motivating and encouraging users to pursue their learning goals.
